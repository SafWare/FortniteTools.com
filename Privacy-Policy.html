API Usage

When you use our tools, we interact with:

• fortniteapi.io
• fortnite-api.com

These services may have their own privacy policies and data collection practices.

Cookies & Storage

We use minimal cookies necessary for website functionality. No personal data is stored locally or on our servers.

Data Security

While we don't collect personal data, we implement standard security measures to protect our website and its users from unauthorized access and potential threats.
